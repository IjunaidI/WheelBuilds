The "Garage"

Customers save every vehicle they own — the daily driver, the project truck, the spouse's SUV. The store remembers them across visits and devices, so every return trip is one tap to "show what fits my truck." It works even before they log in, then follows them into their account.

Fit confirmed on every page

On a product page, the active vehicle gets a clear verdict — fits or doesn't, and why. A wheel that can't physically seat is flagged honestly, before it's ever ordered. That precision is what kills returns and builds trust.

Why this matters

Wrong-fit is the most common reason wheels get returned — and the most common reason buyers don't pull the trigger.

Wheel Builds removes that doubt for every shopper, 24/7, without tying up your phone. Fewer returns, fewer wrong orders, more completed sales — and a buying experience the national chains charge millions to build.
